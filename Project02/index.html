About

Learn more about my journey so far.

1997-2003

Build, Create, Explore

During early development, I had a proclivity for building and creating, from Lego Technic, Mechano, and Lego Mindstorm Robots. I was introduced to computing with Windows XP at an early age and explored operating systems and machine optimization to run early video games.

2003-2015

A New Level

I first encountered coding when I was 12, and created a webpage with HTML. I always had an obsession with technology, and at the age of 15 I built my first computer. I became absorbed by the rapidly evolving hardware landscape and the world of computing.

2015-2021

A Rocky Diversion

At University I studied Earth Science and Geology, which led to a career in mineral exploration. I used SQL database management systems for data collection, and ArcGIS for 3D Modelling and data interpretation. Remote camp work was mentally and physically taxing, and I found myself yearning for something more.

2022

Considering A New Path

The global pandemic and my relocation to Vancouver Island prompted me to reflect on my career path. I came back to my interest in computing, and started learning basic programming from home. I completed Harvard's CS50, and my enthusiasm for computer science continued to grow. I knew I was ready for a career redirection.

2023

Forging A New Path

I enrolled in the Information and Computer Systems Diploma at Camosun and started learning about the world of computing. I am continually developing my skillset including software engineering, web design, and systems administration. I look forward to successful Co-Op placement in the fall of 2023, and opportunities to work on a project team in the technologies space in the future.
